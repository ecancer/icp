## About the side event

This side event at the 79th World Health Assembly will present the 2026 Global Cancer Policy Forum Declaration on Cancer Equity — a set of concrete, costed commitments for closing the global cancer equity gap — and call for its formal adoption into WHO's NCD framework.

## Speakers

- **Professor Richard Sullivan**, Director, Institute of Cancer Policy, King's College London
- **Dr. Miriam Nkosi**, Director of Cancer Services, National Cancer Registry of Nigeria
- **Dr. Priya Sharma**, Director, National Cancer Grid of India
- **H.E. [Minister TBC]**, Ministry of Health, Kenya

## The declaration

The 2026 Declaration calls on WHO member states to commit to:

1. Universal cancer registry coverage by 2030
2. Mandatory LMIC inclusion in oncology clinical trial design
3. A global cancer medicines fund, modelled on COVAX
4. Cancer equity benchmarks within UHC national health plans
5. A permanent WHO cancer equity monitoring mechanism
