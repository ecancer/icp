## About this webinar

Health technology assessment — the process by which national bodies evaluate whether a new medicine should be reimbursed — can seem opaque and inaccessible. This 90-minute webinar demystifies the process and provides practical guidance for those seeking to engage with it.

## Who should attend

- Clinical researchers preparing dossiers for HTA submission
- Patient advocates and charity representatives engaging with NICE, HAS, G-BA, or other national bodies
- Oncology professionals wanting to understand why some drugs reach patients faster than others
- Policy analysts and commissioners
