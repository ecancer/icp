This website is operated by the Institute of Cancer Policy at King's College London. We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

## What data we collect

We collect email addresses submitted via our newsletter sign-up form. We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising. We use privacy-respecting analytics (Plausible Analytics) which collects no personal data.

## How we use your data

Email addresses are used solely to send our newsletter and event invitations. We will never sell or share your data with third parties. You may unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.

## Contact

For data protection enquiries, contact [icp@kcl.ac.uk](mailto:icp@kcl.ac.uk). The King's College London Data Protection Officer can be reached at [dpo@kcl.ac.uk](mailto:dpo@kcl.ac.uk).
