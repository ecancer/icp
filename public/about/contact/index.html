Our three pillars

Research & evidence

We produce world-leading, peer-reviewed research that generates the evidence base policymakers need to act with confidence.

Policy translation

We translate research into clear, actionable recommendations for governments, international bodies, and health systems.

Capacity building

We train the next generation of cancer policy researchers and practitioners, with a particular focus on LMIC institutions.

Field programmes

LMIC field programme

Low- and middle-income countries

Taking cancer policy where it's needed most

More than 70% of cancer deaths occur in low- and middle-income countries, yet most cancer research and policy expertise remains concentrated in the Global North. Our field programmes partner directly with ministries of health, regional cancer networks, and frontline clinicians.
